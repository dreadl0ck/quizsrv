Welcome to the SNE quizzer!


This is a web application I created during my time at OS3, to help train for our exams. We had to memorize a lot of information by heart, and since the human brain learns by repetition the solution seemed obvious: flashcards! Doing this on paper however, soon became tedious and the wish for a digital and collaborative solution emerged. The questions in the SNE quizzer are defined in yaml configurations, the current collection consists of 2018 questions in total.
